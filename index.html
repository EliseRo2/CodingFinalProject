How Much Do Common Prescription Medications Cost in New York?

The cost of prescription medications is a large part of the American Healthcare system. Companies across the world manufacture and sell some of the most commonly prescribed medications used to treat health conditions.

However, the true cost of prescription medications can be a complicated topic to understand. The wholesale acquisition cost (WAC) of a prescription medication is defined as the list price set by pharmaceutical manufacturers. It is mainly for wholesalers and other direct purchasers. The wholesale acquisition cost of a particular medication can vary by state.
